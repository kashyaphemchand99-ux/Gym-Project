What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It's calculated as: weight (kg) / (height (m))²

BMI Categories

Underweight: < 18.5

Focus on building muscle and healthy weight gain

Normal: 18.5 - 24.9

Maintain your healthy lifestyle and fitness routine

Overweight: 25 - 29.9

Start with cardio and strength training programs

Obese: ≥ 30

Consult with our trainers for personalized guidance
